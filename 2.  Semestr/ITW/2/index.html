Tutoring services

I offer mainly online based tutoring. The price for an hour can range between 250 czk to 400 czk, depending on the subject and the level of preparation i would need to make. I specialize in preparing students for university entrance exams, mainly the scio and MUNI TSP ones. My main goal is to teach you great strategy for the exam, mainly for the math part. The most important skills for such exams are quick mathematization of sentences and logical thinking. Learn from me how to best utilize your time on the exam and how to solve most of the types of math problems in these exams.
